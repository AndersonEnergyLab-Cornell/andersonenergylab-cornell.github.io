Katerina's new paper (with Vivek Srikrishnan, and Vivienne Liu) appears in [Cell Reports Sustainability](https://www.cell.com/cell-reports-sustainability/fulltext/S2949-7906(26)00029-7). This paper illustrates how system stress propagates through power grids. This diagnostic perspective helps explain why shortages occur under specific conditions and can complement traditional planning and operational tools to support adaptive reliability strategies, targeted monitoring, and coordinated infrastructure investments.  Congratulations Katerina!
