Join Us

We are an inclusive, interdisciplinary group working at the interface of engineering, applied mathematics, data science and operations research. Join us to work on enabline energy system decarbonization. Students from underrepresented groups and first-generation students are particularly encouraged to apply.

Before applying, please visit the lab manual to make sure that you're comfortable with our mission, values, expectations, and policies.

Graduate Students

We are not currently admitting Ph.D or M.S. students for the 2023-2024 admissions cycle.

Postdoctoral Researchers

We are looking to hire a postdoc to work on energy systems optimization. Particular areas of interest are integrated system modeling, considering coupled infrastructure systems and/or demand response programs, but the successful candidate will have a large degree of freedom in designing their research portfolio. I am also happy to work with you on other postdoctoral opportunities, within Cornell or externally-funded. Please reach out for more information.
